Topic Choice for Final Project

My concept for a final project involves working with the disability services on campus. It would be an interactive map of campus where you can click on a location and see the disability services available (bathrooms, entrances, driving, etc).

If I have the chance to make this project more involved, I would include a search bar where you can search for a given service, and then the parts of campus where it's available will be highlighted, and listed as well.
