How does it work?

  1. Download our app on Google Play or the App Store (Junior does, too!)
  2. Connect us to your calendar app of choice (it automatically syncs up with Junior’s calendar, too.)
  3. Every time something is coming up, you get a reminder!
  4. With the press of a button, you have a regular Re:minder(TM) sent to Junior reminding him/her that there’s something he/she needs to do.
  5. Once the task is done, Junior can press confirm, and you will know immediately!
  6. The app is smart! If Junior does not press ‘confirm’ or ‘in progress’ for more than 15 minutes (you set the time), there is a sensor that activates and shuts down all other apps until confirm is pressed. No procrastinating on our watch!!!
