About Us

We are a team of parents (and future parents) who want to make the complex world of parenting just a little more manageable.

Founded in the heart of Silicon Valley, we at Re:Mind are hoping to harness technology to help parents (and kids) to not have technology not just be a means of division between the generations, but rather a connecting point.

We believe that technology can be the answer, and not always the problem.
